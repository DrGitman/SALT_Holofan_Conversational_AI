# Wise Sayings

This file contains wise sayings in Ju Haonsi with English translations and associated emotions.

## Sayings List

- Original: "A //ha !gum-!gum tsi !qo."
  Translation: "Avoid, shut mouth."
  Emotion: assertive

- Original: "//Au !kau a dsuu."
  Translation: "Thatching, the house properly."
  Emotion: dedicated

- Original: "Da’ma ǂ’ha dhi."
  Translation: "Child's arm is weak."
  Emotion: protective

- Original: "Kahi, tci //hae te ǂhoaǂxa o tsjaa kxao."
  Translation: "That’s why the jackal is regarded as a thief."
  Emotion: cautionary

- Original: "Kaisi ku !gabi tci a ku ho //ku a mama /haá."
  Translation: "Keep on looking back, one will soon see one's grands' eye."
  Emotion: reflective

- Original: "Ka xabe ka /ham khoetsa !gu, tci ha ku ǂhao."
  Translation: "Although it looks like the sun stands still, it will go down."
  Emotion: thoughtful

- Original: "Ka ǂNui-ma !qo /hoa !garo ha /e ko zxoi tci ha ku !gaia ha dzuu !ing."
  Translation: "If a baby vulture doesn’t learn how to fly out from the nest, it will die in the nest."
  Emotion: motivational

- Original: "Ku !nare kxoo dhi."
  Translation: "Remembering the pot."
  Emotion: nostalgic

- Original: "Suu tsaa, ka si!ha sau-á a ko !ghai /hii."
  Translation: "Sleep and they can roast you like a wildebeest’s head."
  Emotion: alert

- Original: "Tca tci !Khau ko du hje kora dzuu."
  Translation: "That’s exactly what the wolf does and don’t have a house."
  Emotion: cautionary

- Original: "Tci kao, mi ku ǂhom a ko ǂhaa."
  Translation: "Listen, if motivated."
  Emotion: open-minded

- Original: "Tsa’ma ǂaán !kau tci ku gu !kxom."
  Translation: "Early bird will catch a worm."
  Emotion: motivated

- Original: "Zam tci ko !haa ǂhao dzhuu."
  Translation: "Tortoise won the race with the ostrich."
  Emotion: inspiring
